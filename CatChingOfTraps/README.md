# Overview

Adds a new religion, **“Cat-Ching of Traps.”**  
This faith grants techniques for making the most of traps.

[![en](https://img.shields.io/badge/lang-en-red.svg)](https://github.com/Seacolor/ElinMOD/blob/main/CatChingOfTraps/README.md)
[![ja](https://img.shields.io/badge/lang-ja-green.svg)](https://github.com/Seacolor/ElinMOD/blob/main/CatChingOfTraps/README.ja.md)

# Details

## Offerings

- Corpse
- Stone
- Sake barrel

## Passive

- Perception
- Spot Hidden
- Disarm Trap

## Bonuses

- Allows the worshipper to see traps.
- Increases the number of traps that appear in Nefia dungeons.

## Rewards

[spoiler]

1. Karakuroid (Pet)
2. Trapper's Bracelet (Artifact)

### Nine-Lives Bakeneko

- Possesses the feat Arcane Core 1.
- Can use Gravity and Drop Trap.
- Drop Trap places a random trap beneath its feet.

### Trapper's Bracelet

- Includes a faction enchantment that enables the detection, avoidance, and utilization of traps.  

[/spoiler]

## Altars

After installing this mod, additional Trap Altars will appear among the altars placed in the fields and in Nefia.
