# Overview

This mod introduces a new faith, **"Cat with Million lives"**.
This faith will protect you and your pets from death.

[![en](https://img.shields.io/badge/lang-en-red.svg)](https://github.com/Seacolor/ElinMOD/blob/main/CatWithMillionLives/README.md)
[![ja](https://img.shields.io/badge/lang-ja-green.svg)](https://github.com/Seacolor/ElinMOD/blob/main/CatWithMillionLives/README.ja.md)

# Details

## Favored Offerings

- Corpses
- bonito flakes
- sake barrel

## Faith Bonuses

- Speed
- Magic
- Stealth
- Pickpocket
- Anatomy

## Faith Ability

- Resurrection of the Soul

### Resurrection of the Soul

- Call an ally back from the world of the dead

## Faith Rewards

1. Nine lives the bake-neko (Pet)
2. Cat's Bell (Artifact)

### Nine lives the bake-neko

- Possesses feats Celestical Cat, Fluffy Tail, Boost, and Levitation.
- When grown by the feet of the Celestical Cat, tails increase, raising speed, mana, and fire resistance (up to a maximum of 7 tails).
- Can use Fire Bolt, Fire Ball, Fire Sword, and Resurrection.

### Cat's Bell

- Faction Enchantments that defends you from death penalty.

## Altars

By installing this mod, water altars will appear among the altars placed in fields and Nefias.
